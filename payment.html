Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What if I need more videos than my plan includes?
You can always upgrade to the next tier, or we can arrange additional videos on a per-project basis. Just reach out through Slack.
Do I need to provide my own footage?
Not necessarily. We can work with raw footage you provide, existing content you want repurposed, or we can help source stock footage and graphics to build videos from scratch.
